Integrated Excise Supply Chain Management System (IESCMS)

A complex application management system designed to streamline the liquor excise supply chain process.

  • Managed application workflows including product requests, approvals, and transport pass generation
  • Integrated RESTful APIs with Angular to process dynamic data for modules such as order pickup, dispatch, stock management, and sales tracking
  • Implemented features for payment deduction, refund processing, and returns, ensuring accuracy and reliability
  • Enabled real-time tracking and monitoring of stock movement across the supply chain from liquor manufacturing to retail shop sales
